A community story

Why three friends started building LocalLift in 2026

It started with a tiffin aunty in Koramangala. She fed the entire street, but had zero online presence — and three new families that moved in had no way to find her.

We realised the most trusted businesses around us were missing from every map and review site. So we built LocalLift: a place where a neighbour's vouch matters more than a star rating, and where the corner shop can be discovered as easily as a chain.

Today thousands of neighbours across India recommend the people they love — and the people they love finally get found.

FAQ

Quick answers

Is LocalLift free for businesses?

Yes — completely free, forever. There are no listing fees, ads, or paid rankings.

How does "neighbour verified" work?

Real people in the same area vouch for a business. The more verified neighbours that vouch, the higher the trust score.

How do I claim my business?

Search your business on Discover. If it's listed, hit "Claim" on the profile and follow the quick verification steps.

How do you handle fake reviews?

We don't have stars — we have neighbour vouches tied to verified local accounts. That makes fakes far harder to manufacture.

Prefer a chat on WhatsApp?

We're a few taps away. Send us a hello and we'll reply same day.
